Charts

1. Exploring COVID-19 Morbidity Trends: An Interactive Visualization.

A chart of global COVID-19 morbidity with our interactive area chart. this visualization offers an exploration of weekly mortality rates spanning from 2020 to 2023.

At the heart of our visualization lies a comprehensive dataset cataloging mortality rates across countries, compiled to paint a vivid picture of the pandemic's toll. Users can navigate through different years and countries, uncovering insights into the shifting patterns of COVID-19.

Through the lens of this chart, users can grasp the profound impact of COVID-19 on global health systems, societies, and economies. By fostering deeper understanding and facilitating data-driven insights, our visualization serves as a valuable tool in navigating the complexities of the pandemic and informing strategic responses to future health crises.

2. Navigating Global Vaccinations: An Interactive Map

Journey through the global landscape of vaccination efforts with our immersive Map. Designed with user exploration in mind, this dynamic visualization empowers users to pan, zoom, and interact with a detailed map of the world, offering a comprehensive view of vaccinations available across countries.

Through intuitive controls, users can navigate the map, zooming in within specific regions or zooming out to gain a broader perspective. By clicking on individual countries, users can access detailed information about vaccinations available and their sources.
